A Wheatfield with Cypresses (occasionally called A Cornfield with Cypresses) is any of three similar 1889 oil paintings by Vincent van Gogh, as part of his wheat field series. All were exhibited at the Saint-Paul-de-Mausole mental asylum at Saint-RÃ©my near Arles, France, where Van Gogh was voluntarily a patient from May 1889 to May 1890.
